Cooking Method

Heat Oil in a pan and add Onions. Cook for 1-2 mins until lightly brown and translucent. In a mixing bowl, add Chicken pieces, Ginger, Garlic, Salt, 2 Tbsp Curry Powder. Mix well. Add Lemon/Lime Juice and mix well. Add Chicken to the pan with onions and mix well. Cover and cook until chicken is done…stirring occasionally. Meanwhile, mix softened Cream Cheese and Broth/Stock with a hand blender and keep aside. When chicken is cooked, add the Cream Cheese sauce, Tomatoes and 1 Tbsp Curry Powder. Mix well and bring to a simmer. Garnish with Cilantro and serve with Rice, Roti, or Bread.

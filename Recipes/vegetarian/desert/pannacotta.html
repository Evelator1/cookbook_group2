How to do it
Ingredients
1 vanilla pod
4 sheets of gelatin
400 g cream
120 g sugar

For the panna cotta, score the vanilla bean and scrape out with the back of a knife. Soak the gelatine in a bowl of cold water for about 5 minutes. Briefly boil the cream and sugar with the vanilla pulp in a saucepan until the sugar has dissolved.

Remove the saucepan from the heat. Lightly squeeze out the soaked gelatine, add to the pot with the still hot (no longer boiling) liquid. Stir briefly until the gelatine has dissolved. Prepare 4 small bowls (approx. 125 ml each).

Pour in the liquid panna cotta and let the bowls cool for about 10-15 minutes. Then refrigerate the dessert for at least 5 hours, preferably overnight.
